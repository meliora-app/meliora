About Us

In the midst of a pandemic where people — old and young alike — are constantly navigating through challenges, mental health has become a major concern. In fact, mental health issues are beginning to deteriorate an individual’s value system. Nowadays, in a distressing situation, People are more likely to act in an unethical and unprofessional manner.

While several existing products like Vent and Therapy Buddy attempt at improving mental intelligence, they have a major shortcoming. Users cannot share their thoughts and experiences, expecting to hear positive feedback. Mainstream apps like these do not aim to filter out negative behavior, and don’t necessarily encourage a positive mindset within the original blogger. In addition, members cannot interact with their communities either through a written method or a visual method.

This is why our team built Meliora: a blogging app that never ceases to inspire and motivate you.

Features

Find Purpose

Share your thoughts and emotions by creating social posts describing your experiences and how you overcame it. Be the person promote positivity and good values in others.

In Meliora, you can also react to the posts of others through captivating emojis (hearts, smiley faces, thumbs up, etc.)

In Meliora, you can also post anonymously to protect your privacy.

Follow the Good

Meliora provides a follow feature to enables users to follow other members in their community . You can also follow broader categories like exam-motivation through the homepage of the app.

In addition, as a complementary feature, Meliora also displays the most popular posts for a given week in the homepage based on the number of reactions made by users on a given post.

Flag the Bad

See something disturbing? Flag it and we will ensure that the rest of the Meliora community does not see it. Any negative or disheartening posts can be reported using the flagging link attached to every post in the Meliora app.

We, the developers of Meliora want to ensure that every user has a positive experience and through our software we give you the freedom to help us in achieving this mission.

Community

As a college student you may be facing various problems. Starting from roommate issues and ending with graduating problems, Meliora can be your best friend. Reading about how other students overcame such problems or writing a new post by yourself can help boost your confidence.

Who knows? Maybe the person you follow on Meliora could end up being your future roommate

You may be a medical patient with a chronic illness or you could be recovering from pain. If this is the case, Meliora is the pill for you.

Studies by psychologists have shown that sharing your experience helps reduce pain. Especially, during this pandemic, Meliora can be your source of comfort.

Having relationship problems? Want to discuss them with someone? Then, the community in Meliora can help Whether it is personal issues or professional issues, you can write about them in Meliora.

Everyone has gone through some kind of relationship issue or the other. You can benefit from others in the Meliora community.
